What happened in Han Dynasty?

  • There are few remains of Han-era houses except for their foundations but what houses were like at that time can be ascertained from models of houses buried with dead.

What did Han Dynasty do?

  1. The Han Dynasty ruled China from 206 B.C. to 220 A.D. and was the second imperial dynasty of China. Though tainted by deadly dramas within the royal court, it is also known for its trade route to Europe, permanently altering the course of Chinese history.
